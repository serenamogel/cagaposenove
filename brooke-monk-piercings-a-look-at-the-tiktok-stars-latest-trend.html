A Look at the TikTok Star's Latest Trend

Brooke Monk is a rising star on TikTok, known for her dance videos, comedy skits, and engaging personality. But in addition to her entertaining content, Monk has also become known for her love of piercings. From her helix to her nose, Monk has been sharing her latest piercings with her followers, and the trend has taken off.

In a recent video, Monk took her followers on a tour of her piercings, showing off her latest additions and sharing her experience with each one. From the initial pain to the healing process, Monk gives an honest and detailed account of what it’s like to get a piercing.

But Monk doesn’t just get piercings for the sake of it. In another video, she let her Instagram followers choose her next piercing, giving them a choice between a septum, a smiley, and a daith. The verdict? A septum piercing, which Monk proudly showed off in a follow-up video.

While some may criticize Monk for her love of piercings, she sees it as a form of self-expression and a way to feel more confident in her own skin. And with her growing popularity on TikTok, Monk is inspiring others to embrace their own unique style and express themselves in their own way.

So whether you’re a fan of piercings or not, there’s no denying that Brooke Monk is making a statement with her latest trend. And with her infectious personality and engaging content, it’s no wonder she’s quickly becoming one of TikTok’s biggest stars.
