Sasha Banks Talks Secrets From Star Wars Mandalorian Season Two

Star Wars’ Mandalorian is coming back for season two on Disney Plus and Sasha Banks will be a part of the action in a galaxy far, far away. This is a role she’s obviously excited about. It is also a bit surreal and it comes with a ton of secrets.

Sasha Banks recently spoke to The New Day: Feel The Power podcast where her role in the Mandalorian came up. She said that it doesn’t even feel real yet, and there is a lot that she’s not even allowed to talk about yet.

“It still has not hit me. There is not much I can talk about yet. All I can say is I’m in it. I can’t believe it. I’m so excited and can’t wait for everybody to see it. Season 2 is going to be so incredible. I have not seen the episodes. I probably won’t even know what’s going on actually (until it airs). It’s so secretive.”

Sasha Banks will likely have a lot more to talk about when Mandalorian’s second season drops. Until then she’s keeping a lid on things.
