Who is Amy Winehouse husband Blake Fielder-Civil?

Amy Jade Winehouse was an English singer and songwriter who passed away on July 23, 2011. She was renowned for both her wide range of musical styles, which included soul, jazz, and rhythm and blues, as well as her rich, expressive contralto voice.

Winehouse, who was a young member of the National Youth Jazz Orchestra, joined Simon Fuller’s 19 Management in 2002. She quickly began recording songs before agreeing to a publishing deal with EMI. Through these record labels, she also established a professional working relationship with producer Salaam Remi. Frank, Winehouse’s self-titled debut album, was released in 2003. Apart from two covers, most of the album’s songs had jazz influences and were co-written by Winehouse.

Who is Amy Winehouse husband Blake Fielder-Civil?

Amy Winehouse’s ex-husband and former assistant on music videos is Blake Fielder-Civil. He has a criminal past, and there are rumors that he contributed to Amy Winehouse’s heroin addiction. Amy Winehouse and Fielder-Civil were only married for a few years, but their union was a pivotal time in her life that unfortunately ended much too soon. In May 2007, they finally wed in a discreet ceremony in Miami.

The couple split up after Fielder-Civil received a 27-month prison term for attempting to tamper with the course of justice and causing great bodily damage.

Later, Blake Fielder-Civil married Sarah Aspin, his current spouse. Before meeting Amy, he was already intimately linked with Aspin, with whom he shares two children.
