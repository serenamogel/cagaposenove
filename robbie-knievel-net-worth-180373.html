Robbie Knievel Net Worth

Robbie Knievel was born on May 7, 1962 in Butte, MT. He was the son of legendary stuntman and daredevil Evel Knievel. Robbie followed in his father's footsteps and became a stuntman and daredevil himself, performing stunts all over the world. He was known for his death-defying jumps and thrilling performances.
