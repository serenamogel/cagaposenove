Report: Update On Triple Hs Absence From WWE RAW, RAW Media Rights Talks

WWE Chief Content Officer Triple H was reportedly absent from the November 27 episode of WWE RAW. He was said to be handling “other WWE business”, and a new report provides an update.

Mike Johnson of PWInsider reports that, per several sources, Triple H and Nick Khan have been in Los Angeles over the past few days. They are said to have been working on the media rights deal for WWE RAW. RAW’s media rights are set to come up in 2024. The report notes that there are several suitors at this point.

In recent months, WWE has landed new deals for WWE SmackDown and NXT. SmackDown will return to USA Network and NXT will move to The CW Network in October 2024.

Ari Emanuel recently stated that TKO is having productive talks with multiple parties about WWE RAW. Check out his comments here.

WrestleZone will monitor the situation and provide more information as it becomes available.
