Salma Hayek Biography, Height, Weight, And Everything You Need To Know

Salma Hayek is a Mexican-American actress and producer who has made a significant impact in the film industry. Here’s some information about her biography, height, weight, and other key details:

Biography:

  • Full Name: Salma Hayek Pinault
  • Date of Birth: September 2, 1966
  • Age: She is currently 56 years old.
  • Place of Birth: Coatzacoalcos, Veracruz, Mexico
  • Nationality: Mexican and American

Career Highlights:

  • Salma Hayek gained recognition for her breakout role in the Mexican telenovela “Teresa” in 1989.
  • She then transitioned to Hollywood and became known for her work in films such as “Desperado” (1995), “From Dusk Till Dawn” (1996), and “Frida” (2002).
  • Hayek received critical acclaim and earned an Academy Award nomination for her portrayal of the Mexican artist Frida Kahlo in the film “Frida.”

Height and Weight:

  • Height: Salma Hayek stands approximately 5 feet 2 inches tall (157 cm).
  • Weight: Her weight has varied throughout her career, but it is estimated to be around 125-130 pounds (57-59 kg).

Other Notable Facts:

  • Salma Hayek is multilingual and speaks Spanish, English, French, and Portuguese fluently.
  • She has been involved in humanitarian work and activism, supporting various causes such as women’s rights, violence against women, and UNICEF.
  • In 2009, Hayek married French billionaire François-Henri Pinault, the CEO of Kering, a luxury goods company. They have a daughter named Valentina Paloma Pinault.
