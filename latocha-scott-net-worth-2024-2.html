Early Life and Career Beginnings

Latocha Scott was born on October 2, 1973, in Atlanta, Georgia. She began her music career at a young age, singing in church choirs and local talent shows. In the early 1990s, she formed the R&B group Xscape with her sister Tamika Scott and friends Kandi Burruss and Tameka “Tiny” Cottle.

Rise to Fame with Xscape

Xscape was discovered by producer Jermaine Dupri while performing at a music festival in Atlanta. Dupri signed the group to his So So Def label, and they released their debut album “Hummin’ Comin’ at ‘Cha” in 1993. The album was a commercial success, earning platinum status and spawning two top 10 singles on the Billboard Hot 100.

Solo Career and Acting

After Xscape disbanded in the early 2000s, Scott embarked on a solo career. She released her debut solo album, “Solo Flight 404,” in 2004. In addition to her music career, Scott has also ventured into acting, appearing in films and television shows such as “Fair Game” and “Living Single.”

Return to Xscape and Continued Success

In 2017, Scott reunited with the original members of Xscape for a tour and a reality show, “Xscape: Still Kickin’ It.” The group’s return was well-received by fans, further adding to Scott’s wealth and fame.
