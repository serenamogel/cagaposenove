About Adam Shapiro

Adam Shapiro is a talented actor known for starring in the films “Steve Jobs (2015), Never Have I Ever (2020) and She Said (2022).”

Shapiro’s career cuts across screen and stage acting, and is most famous for appearing as Jesse Tyler, in the popular TV series Scandal.

The American-born film star grew up at the outskirts of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, and graduated from the University of Maryland.

In addition, Shapiro appeared in a few short films like The Interview, Short Term 12, and Jewz in the Hood.

Shapiro is the owner of the Philadelphia soft pretzel company Shappy Pretzel Co.
