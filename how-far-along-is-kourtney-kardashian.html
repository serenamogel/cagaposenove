How far along is Kourtney Kardashian

How far along is Kourtney Kardashian?

The due date for Kourtney Kardashian’s pregnancy has not been publicly disclosed.

However, the reality star surprised her husband, Travis Barker, with the happy news during a recent Blink-182 concert.

During the concert, Kourtney, dressed in a black long-sleeve bodysuit, held up a white sign with the words “Travis I’m Pregnant” written on it.

Fellow bandmate Mark Hoppus brought the sign to Travis’ attention by repeatedly shouting his name and gesturing toward Kourtney.

Both Travis and Mark paused their performance, stepping down from the stage to give Kourtney a hug, and Travis even planted a big kiss on her.

This will be Kourtney’s first child with Travis, while she already has three children (Mason, Penelope, and Reign) from her previous relationship with Scott Disick.

Similarly, Travis has two children named Landon and Alabama from his past marriage to Shanna Moakler.

Regrettably, the authenticity of this information cannot be confirmed without verification from the reality television star herself or her doctor.

Since it is common for women not to announce their pregnancy until after the first trimester, it is reasonable to assume that Kourtney is at least three months pregnant.

Consequently, the baby could potentially be born anytime between September 2023 and early winter.

The estimated due date would likely fall somewhere between mid-October and mid-December.

However, without official confirmation, speculations about the exact timing remain uncertain.
