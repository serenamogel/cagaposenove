Ryan Edwards &amp; His Girlfriend Thrown Out of Bar After Brawl

Credit: MTV

It does not look like sober living is going too well…After his girlfriend Amanda Conner got into an altercation at a bar, she and Ryan Edwards were thrown out of the building. Unfortunately for them, it’s on video.

The Teen Mom star has been dating Amanda since they met in rehab earlier this year. Their odd relationship has been the subject of many headlines.

Not only were Ryan Edwards and Amanda Conner spotted going out to a bar, but also they were thrown out after Ryan’s girlfriend got into a messy brawl.

Amanda is a single mother who unfortunately landed in the CADAS rehab on her latest charges of driving under the influence and possession of a controlled substance.

It does not seem like the two have managed to keep each other out of trouble.

The Sun obtained video of Ryan being kicked out of the bar by bouncers. The MTV star is shouting in the footage. It all happened around 10:30 pm on Saturday November 25th

His 33-year-old girlfriend is also getting removed from the premises in the clip.

According to the outlet, the 16 & Pregnant alum lives in a halfway house on rehab property that he is allowed to sign out of. That presumably means that he also needs to stay sober to avoid any more trouble with the law.

An eyewitness reports that Ryan seemed “out of it” and was “slurring his words.”

Someone explained to The Sun how it all went down.

“Ryan and Amanda were with a group of people,” they detailed. “At one point, one of the guys in their group went up to Amanda and grabbed her by the face.”
