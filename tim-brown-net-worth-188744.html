Tim Brown Net Worth

Tim Brown is a former NFL wide receiver who was born on July 22, 1966 in Dallas, TX. He is best known for winning the 1987 Heisman Trophy while playing for Notre Dame and for his long career with the Oakland Raiders. During his time in the NFL, he was selected for 9 Pro Bowls and amassed 1,094 receptions, 14,934 receiving yards and 105 touchdowns.
