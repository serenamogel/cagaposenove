About

Linda Blair is a 65 year old American Actress. Born Linda Denise Blair on 22nd January, 1959 in St. Louis, Missouri, USA, she is famous for her role as Regan MacNeil in the horror film The Exorcist (1973), for which she won a Golden Globe Award and received a nomination for an Academy Award in a career that spans 1962–present. Her zodiac sign is Aquarius.

Martin Sheen is a 83 year old American Actor. Born Ramon Gerardo Antonio Estevez on 3rd August, 1940 in Dayton, Ohio, United States, he is famous for President Bartlet on The West Wing in a career that spans 1961–present. His zodiac sign is Leo.
