EX FireRed &amp; LeafGreen (TCG)

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Pokémon TCG: EX FireRed & LeafGreen (Japanese: 伝説の飛翔 Flight of Legends) is the name given to the sixth main expansion of cards from the EX Series of the Pokémon Trading Card Game. It is the first main expansion from the PCG Era of the Pokémon Card Game in Japan. The set continues to feature Pokémon-ex in the card game.

Blurb

BRACE YOURSELF FOR AN ALL-NEW ERA OF BATTLE!

Get ready for an epic adventure. Collect Pokémon from the Kanto region and send them head-to-head against Pokémon from Hoenn. Capture them. Train them. And, move one step closer to becoming a Master Trainer!

Information

EX FireRed & LeafGreen is the name given to the sixth main expansion of the EX Series of the Pokémon Trading Card Game. In Japan, it was released as Flight of Legends, the first expansion in the Pokémon Card Game PCG Era. It is based on Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen, featuring Generation I Pokémon and the Kanto region. The English expansion was released on August 30, 2004, while the Japanese expansion was released on April 9, 2004.

Languages this set is released in

The Flight of Legends set is only released in Japanese, in both 1st and unlimited edition. The EX FireRed & LeafGreen set is released in English, French, German, and Italian, with cards also available as Reverse Holofoils. The non-holo Uncommons in the Japanese, English, French, German, and Italian sets are available with two different codes at the bottom-left; the non-holo Commons in the Japanese set are available with four different codes at the bottom-left; and the non-Holo Commons in the English, French, German, and Italian sets have three different codes available. The purpose of these different codes is still unknown, but occurred throughout the first halve of the EX era.
