Gabrielle Carteris Siblings: Meet James Carteris

Gabrielle Anne Carteris, an American actress who was born on January 2, 1961, is a powerful figure in the union movement. Andrea Zuckerman’s portrayal in the cherished television series Beverly Hills, 90210 is by far her most well-known and identifiable performance, despite the fact that she has established herself in a number of acting roles.

As soon as Carteris agreed to take the prestigious position of executive vice president at SAG-AFTRA in 2012, her journey into the union world had officially begun. This particular union was formed by the merger of the Screen Actors Guild (SAG) and the American Federation of Television and Radio Artists (AFTRA).

As fate would have it, Carteris was forced to assume even more responsibility inside the organization on March 23, 2016, the day Ken Howard, the union’s president, passed away. As a result, she took on the duties of acting president and was elected to the position.

On April 9, 2016, the union’s national board chose Carteris to fill the remaining two years of Howard’s two-year term as president, praising her leadership skills and commitment. This marked a significant turning point in her career and solidified her position as a leading figure in the industry.

Gabrielle Carteris Siblings: Meet James Carteris

James Carteris is Gabrielle’s twin brother. Her father is of Greek descent, and her mother is Jewish. However, her parents divorced just six months after she was born. Following the divorce, Gabrielle’s mother traveled to San Francisco, California, with her and her brother, where she opened a children’s clothes store.
