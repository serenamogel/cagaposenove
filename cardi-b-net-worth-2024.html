Early Life and Career Beginnings

Cardi B’s journey to fame and fortune began in The Bronx, New York, where she was born and raised. Her early life was marked by hardship and her determination to rise above her circumstances. She first gained public attention through social media with her bold and candid posts about her life as a stripper. This platform eventually led to her casting on the reality television series “Love & Hip Hop: New York,” where her vibrant personality quickly made her a fan favorite.

Breakthrough in Music

Cardi B’s music career took off with the release of her debut single “Bodak Yellow,” which soared to the top of the charts and became a defining anthem of 2017. The success of “Bodak Yellow” and subsequent hits from her debut album “Invasion of Privacy” solidified her status as a rap superstar. Her music, characterized by its raw and unapologetic style, has earned her numerous awards and accolades, contributing significantly to her net worth.

Album Sales and Streaming Revenue
