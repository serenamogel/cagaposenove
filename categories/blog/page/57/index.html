published on August 11, 2024
SHOPPERS who have purchased a popular TGI Friday's at-home snack may be eligible for compensation from a settlement. The company that makes the frozen TGI Friday's Mozzarella Sticks Snacks has agreed to pay $900,000 due to false advertising. A lawsuit alleged that Inventure Foods advertised the snacks contained real mozzarella cheese when they did not, violating the Illinois Consumer Fraud Act, according to Top Class Actions.
