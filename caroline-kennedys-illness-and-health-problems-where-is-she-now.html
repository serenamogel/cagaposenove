Caroline Kennedys Illness And Health Problems: Where Is She Now?

The newly appointed American ambassador to Canada, Caroline Kennedy, captivated the attention of the citizens after news of her health issue surfaced on the Internet.

Coming from a family of American politicians, Caroline Kennedy is the only living child of former President John F. Kennedy and First Lady Jacqueline Kennedy.

She is an attorney, author, and diplomat from America who has served as the current United States Ambassador to Australia since 2022.

President Joe Biden nominated Caroline to be the next United States Ambassador to Australia in May 2021. A year later, she was confirmed as the Senate and officially sworn in as the United States Ambassador to Australia On June 10, 2022.

Before serving as Ambassador to Australia, Caroline got appointed to be the United States Ambassador to Japan by President Barack Obama in 2013.

After she was appointed as the next U.S. Ambassador to Japan, she resumed her ambassador role and duties in November 2013 and served until she resigned in January 2017, upon the inauguration of Donald Trump.
