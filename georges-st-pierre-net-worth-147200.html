Georges St-Pierre Net Worth

Georges St-Pierre is a Canadian mixed martial artist who was born on May 19, 1981 in Saint-Isidore, Canada. He is widely considered one of the greatest welterweights in UFC history, having won three UFC titles and going 12-2 in title fights during his career. He is renowned for his technical striking and grappling skills, and is considered one of the greatest MMA fighters of all time.

Before Fame

Bullied in school, he learned Kyokushin karate from his father when he was seven years old. He worked as a bouncer and garbageman before he went pro.

Trivia

He was named Fighter of the Year by several publications in 2009 and was nominated for an ESPY for Best Fighter in 2008, 2010, and 2011.
