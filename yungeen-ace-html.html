Yungeen Ace - Age, Family, Bio

Florida rapper who was known as a member of the "Yungeen" gang. He is represented by Cinematic Music Group and is most recognized for his hit single "Gorillaz."

Before Fame

He first began releasing tracks to Soundcloud in March of 2018. His first release was titled "No Witness." He followed the track up a few weeks later with his popular hit "All In."

Trivia

His music video for the single "All In" was featured on World Star Hip Hop's YouTube channel as exclusive content. 

Family Life

He was born Keyanta Bullard in East Chicago, Indiana and relocated to Jacksonville, Florida at a young age. He was involved in a tragic shooting in 2018 but survived. He co-runs the couples channel Ace And Chloe alongside his girlfriend Chloe.

Associated With

He is known for having collaborated with fellow rapper JayDaYoungan on his single "Jungle."
